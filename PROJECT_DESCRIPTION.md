# VerifyEU - Project Overview

## What is VerifyEU?

VerifyEU is a digital verification tool designed for politicians and electoral campaigns to establish and prove the authenticity of their official images and media. It creates a permanent, tamper-proof record that proves:

- **When** an image was officially published by the campaign
- **Who** published the image (verified through the campaign account)
- **If a circulating image matches officially registered campaign content**

## The Problem We're Solving

Elections are increasingly under attack from deepfakes and manipulated images. Voters see images online claiming to show a politician saying or doing something damaging—but how can they know if the image is real?

**Election integrity is threatened when:** - **Deepfakes** show politicians saying things they never said
- **Edited images** twist the context of real events
- **Out-of-context photos** are spread as current "breaking news" from years ago
- **Voters can't verify** what content is actually from the campaign
- **Misinformation spreads faster than fact-checks** can debunk it
- **Election authorities lack tools** to certify authentic campaign materials

## How It Works (Simple Version)

1. **Register Campaign Content**: Politicians and campaigns upload their official images and videos
   - Creates a permanent cryptographic fingerprint of the content
   - Establishes an official timestamp and source
   - Signs with a verified endorsement that only the campaign can produce

2. **Combat Misinformation**: When fake or manipulated versions appear online
   - Users upload suspicious images
   - VerifyEU instantly shows what the campaign actually released
   - Proves if an image has been altered or is completely fabricated
   - Displays side-by-side comparisons of real vs. fake versions

## What Makes This Different?

Other approaches to image verification are either:
- **Manual** - Slow, human error prone
- **AI-Driven** - Untrustworthy, slow

Our system is:
- **Automatic** - Just upload, get instant results
- **Neutral** - Leaves the final decision to the user, only gives them the tools to decide
- **Cryptographically proven** - Can't be faked by anyone
- **Simple** - Designed for anyone to use
